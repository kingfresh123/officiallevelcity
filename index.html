Level City Camp History

On 8 June 2025, Level City Camp organized its first Peace Walk and cleanup exercise in Boli. The event promoted unity, love, and development in the community. Youth, leaders, and residents marched through the streets carrying peace messages.

The event was supported by DJ 10Plus, Toppie Junior, Boora, and the CEO of XPRESS GH ONLINE. The program ended with speeches encouraging unity and development in the Upper West Region.
